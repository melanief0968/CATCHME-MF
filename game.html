Rules :
·Catch Me is a two players game.
·The Runner has to cross the board and the Catcher has to inercept him.
·Catcher can create walls to slow down the opponant but the catcher can destroy those walls in his vertical or horizontal way to break the whole line
·but with this power he may reveal his position...
·here's an advice : use it with parsimony!

Play :
·Catcher starts at the top of the board with 3 pawns in position 3,5,7.
·Runner take a random place at the bottom of the board.
·Neither player is visible to the other.
·The players take turns.
·They can move by selecting a pawn or use a power.
·Walls for the Catcher, vertical or horizontal explosion.
·Catcher wins if he finds Runner with one of his pawns.
·Runner wins if he reach the opposite side.
